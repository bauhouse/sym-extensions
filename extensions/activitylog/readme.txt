Activity Log
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Version: 1.0.1
Author: Rowan Lewis <rowan@pixelcarnage.com>
Build Date: 19 May 2009
Requirements: Symphony 2.0


Installation
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.	Upload the 'activitylog' folder in this archive to your Symphony
	'extensions' folder.
	
3.	Enable it by selecting "Activity Log", choose Enable from the with-selected
	menu, then click Apply.
	
4.	There is now an "Activity Log" item in the "System" menu.
