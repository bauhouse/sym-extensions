New Visiter
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Version: 1.0
Author: Mark Lewis <mark@casadelewis.com>
Build Date: 12 April 2009
Requirements: Symphony 2.0.1

Installation
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Upload the 'new_vister' folder in this archive to your Symphony
   'extensions' folder.

2. Enable it by selecting the "New Visiter" item under Extensions, choose Enable
   from the with-selected menu, then click Apply.

3. Read the documentation on http://symphony-cms.com.
