
Map Location Field
------------------------------------

Version: 1.0
Author: Symphony Team (team@symphony21.com)
Build Date: 1st Feb 2008
Requirements: Symphony Beta revision 4 or greater.


[INSTALLATION]

1. Upload the 'maplocationfield' folder in this archive to your Symphony 'extensions' folder.

2. Enable it by selecting the "Map Location Field extension", choose Enable from the with-selected menu, then click Apply.

3. Go to System > Preferences and enter your "Google Map API" key.

4. You can now add the "Map Location Field" to your sections.

5. Clap your hands like a monkey!
