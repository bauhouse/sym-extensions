SEO Manager
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Version: 1.0.3
Author: Rowan Lewis <rowan@pixelcarnage.com>
Build Date: 30 June 2009
Requirements: Symphony 2.0


Installation
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.	Upload the 'seomanager' folder in this archive to your Symphony
	'extensions' folder.

2.	Enable it by selecting the "SEO Manager", choose Enable from the
	with-selected menu, then click Apply.

3.	Add the "SEO Manager: Get Rule" event to your pages, and create your rules.
