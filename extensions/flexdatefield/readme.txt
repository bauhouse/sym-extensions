Field: Flex Date
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Version: 1.004
Author: Rowan Lewis <rowan@pixelcarnage.com>
Build Date: 19 March 2009
Requirements: 2.0.1


Installation
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.	Upload the 'flexdatefield' folder in this archive to your Symphony
	'extensions' folder.

2.	Enable it by selecting the "Field: Flex Date", choose Enable from the
	with-selected menu, then click Apply.

4. You can now add the "Flex Date" field to your sections.
