Field: Curl Input
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Version: 1.0.4
Author: Rowan Lewis <rowan@pixelcarnage.com>
Build Date: 30 March 2009
Requirements: Symphony 2


Installation
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Upload the 'curlinputfield' folder in this archive to your Symphony
   'extensions' folder.

2. Enable it by selecting the "Field: Curl Input", choose Enable from the
   with-selected menu, then click Apply.

3. You can now add the "Curl Input" field to your sections.
