Field: Date Tag List
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Version: 1.002
Author: Rowan Lewis <rowan@pixelcarnage.com>
Build Date: 05 December 2008
Requirements: Symphony 2 beta 5


Installation
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.	Upload the 'datetaglistfield' folder in this archive to your Symphony
	'extensions' folder.

2.	Enable it by selecting the "Field: Date Tag List", choose Enable from the
	with-selected menu, then click Apply.

4. You can now add the "Date Tag List" field to your sections.
